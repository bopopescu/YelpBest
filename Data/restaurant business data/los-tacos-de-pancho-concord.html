      Specialties

      Our business specializes in burritos, tacos, enchiladas, tamales. Our meats include Carnitas, Al Pastor, Carne Asada, Grilled Chicken, Buche, Lengua. The salsas are really good as well. Everything is prepared in house and we use nothing but the best ingredients. We also offer grilled tilapia fish tacos, shrimp burritos, and a Surf & Turf burrito that I invented called Boby Burrito. Our Machaca is very flavorful and authentic, not like other restaurants that try to sell it as shredded beef. We offer pork and chicken tamales. If you have a business event let us know and we will prepare your order.

      History

      Established in 2002.

      The restaurant was established by a family of La Soledad, Michoacan;   Francisco Lopez, Maria del Carmen Lopez, Genaro Lopez & Nancy Lopez in 2002. The menu is based on Michoacano recipes. They have been Concord residents for over 30 years and are very active in the local business community.
